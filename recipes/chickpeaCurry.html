Chickpea Curry

Chickpea Curry under 30 minutes

This is a classic amongst the traditional cousine of India and is one of my favorite recipes when I'm short of time or when I want to cook a lot of for the rest of food the week.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 2 onions, minced
  • 2 clover garlic, minced
  • 2 teaspoons fresh ginger root, finely chopped
  • 6 whole cloves
  • 2 (2 inch) stick cinnamon, crushed
  • 1 teaspoon grund cumin
  • 1 teaspoon ground coriander
  • salt
  • 1 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 1 teaspoon ground turmeric
  • 2 (15 ounce) cans chickpea beans
  • 1 cup chopped fresh cilantro
