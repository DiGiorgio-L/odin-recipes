Brenda's Lasagna

Brenda's Lasagna under 30 minutes

The lasagna is, probably, one of the most popular italian dishes and some would say that is kinda overrated, but if you are one of those, this recipe is going to make you change your mind.

Ingredients

  • 1 (16 ounce) package lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 (16 ounce) jar spaghetti sauce
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1/2 pound shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 pound shredded Cheddar cheese
  • 1 pint ricotta cheese

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add pasta and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until al dente; drain.
  2. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). In a large skillet over medium-high heat, brown beef and season with salt and pepper; drain. Stir the spaghetti sauce and garlic and simmer 5 minutes.
  3. In a medium bowl, combine mozzarella, Cheddar and ricotta; stir well. In 9x13 inch pan, alternate layers of noodles, meat mixture and cheese mixture until pan is filled.
  4. Bake in preheat oven for 30 minutes, or until cheese is melted and blubby.
